How To: Get 200 tokens for increasing your spending limit

AuraCandy February 24, 2021

Why Chaturbate gives out 200 tokens for free

Long story short — to prevent fraud. Fraudulent tokens cost Chaturbate money. When someone pays with stolen money it hurts everyone involved. To prevent losses Chaturbate puts a tight limit on your spending. Filling forms doesn't sound fun. To motivate you to fill the form Chaturbate rewards you 200 tokens.

How do I know that is legit? 

Don't trust me. Trust Chaturbate. Visit the page where Chaturbate tells about the deal: How can I receive tokens free.

I want those 200 tokens. How do I get them?

You'll do this in 3 steps, it'll take five to ten minutes. You'll need a pen, your ID, a printer and a phone with a camera or a scanner.

🖨️ Step 1 — download and print the form

  1. Register on Chaturbate, if you haven't already.
  2. Visit the How can I receive tokens free page.
  3. Download the "Spending Limit Increase Form" PDF the page links to. Right-click the link and choose the "Save link as...".
  4. Print the sucker how you'd print any other document.

You're doing great 👏

🖊️Step 2 — fill the form

Read the form and fill it. What goes where:

  1. Customer name — your full legal name. 
  2. Billing address — your full billing address for the payment method. 
  3. Email — your email Chaturbate staff can use to contact you.
  4. Phone — your phone number (don't worry, I never heard Chaturbate call anyone)
  5. Put your signature and date on it.

Almost there 💪

📨Step 3 — scan and send (no scanner — no worries)

  1. Scan or take a photo of the completed form
  2. Scan or take a photo of your ID (state issued or passport)
  3. Send the above documents to support@chaturbate.com using your mail client. Any subject will do but I suggest putting "Spending Limit Increase Request"
  4. Wait for Chaturbate Support to confirm your request. Can take up to 48 hours but often happens faster.

Done. You're awesome. 🙌 Members who've purchased and have their spending limit increased will be given 200 tokens free of charge. Keep being awesome ✨
